Admission Eligibility

Challenger Basic School accepts students with no specific school boundaries. Because our goal is to set each of our students up for a successful school experience while maintaining healthy self-esteem, we highly encourage our new parents to familiarize themselves with our philosophy, curriculum, and expectations. Our intent is to assure students are ready for our rigorous curriculum and structured environment.

Enrollment Procedure

  • New students are accepted on a first come, first served basis.
  • Children with siblings already attending Challenger Basic School do have first priority.
  • Enrollment after a class is full is based on a lottery system. You will receive an email or phone call stating if your child has been accepted or is on the wait list.
  • Students are officially enrolled when all enrollment paperwork has been processed including: proof of residency, all updated immunizations, and one of the following:

In requesting documentation of a student's age and identity, Arizona law allows for a number of alternatives:

  1. A certified copy of the student's birth certificate;
  2. Other reliable proof of the student's identity and age, including the pupil's baptismal certificate, an application for a social security number or original school registration records and an affidavit explaining the inability to provide a copy of the birth certificate; or
  3. A letter from the authorized representative of an agency having custody of the student pursuant to A.R.S, Title 8, Chapter 2 certifying that the student has been placed in the custody of the agency as prescribed by law. (A.R.S.§ 15-828(A))

Kindergarten

Children must be 5 years old before September 1 for kindergarten eligibility. We do not offer a test-in option for students who are too young. CBS offers two options for Kindergarten: half-day and all-day. ** Parents will have the first week to decide which option their child will attend.
